SOP OF VOCATIONAL TRAINING

Objective: To ensure that students have the necessary documents for reporting to the Learning & Development Centre.

Procedure:

  • a. C&A (Character & Antecedent) Certificate:
  • The C&A Certificate should include the Undertaking Form certified by the respective Heads of Departments (HODs).
  • The C&A Certificate should have a recent photograph of the student with a seal by the respective HOD.
  • b. Acceptance Letter:
  • The student must bring two copies of the Acceptance Letter provided to them.
  • c. Original Identity Card:
  • The student must bring the original Identity Card provided by their Institute/College.
  • Two photocopies of the Identity Card should also be brought.
  • d. Personal Insurance Copy:
  • The student must provide a copy of their personal insurance policy with a minimum value of rupees one lakh (Ref. no.5).
  • e. Passport Size Photographs:
  • Two passport size color photographs should be brought for the security Gate Pass.
  • f. Address Proof:
  • The student must bring a copy of their address proof, which can be any of the following: Voter ID, Aadhar Card

    TERMS AND CONDITION

    Term and Conditions:

    • 1. The trainee(s) must be made aware of potential hazards at Barauni Refinery such as Major Accident Explosions, Deflagration and Detonation, Confined and Unconfined Vapor Cloud Explosions, Toxic Releases, Fire, Boiling Liquid Expanding Vapor Explosion, etc.
    • 2. No transport, boarding, lodging, stipend shall be provided to the student(s) during the training period.
    • 3. Trainee(s) are to observe working schedule as under: Working Days: Monday to Friday, Time: (10.00 am to 5.00 pm)
    • 4. Trainee(s) must have him/her safety/leather shoes and Safety helmet on before entering the Refinery premises.
    • 5. Trainee(s) must Insure him/herself under accidental insurance cover minimum sum of Rs.1 lac by any Insurance Company.
    • 6. The trainee(s) will maintain a diary for his/her day to day work and will produce the same to the L&D Centre as and when asked for, duly signed by the officer of the concerned section.
    • 7. The trainee(s) to produce a project report to the L&D Centre, for being qualified to be issued a training completion certificate.
    • 8. The trainee(s) will have to abide by the rules and regulations of the corporation as enforced and amended from time to time.
    • 9. Extending the training facility to the Vocational Trainee will not confer any right on the trainee for employment in the Corporation.
    • 10. Minimum CGPA % marks in Vth Semester - 5.0 / 50%
    • 11. Pen Drive, Laptop, and Mobile phones are NOT ALLOWED in Refinery.
